# Boson Stars and Gravastars

My early research focused on compact-object solutions sourced by scalar fields,
including boson stars and gravastar-like configurations. This work involved
Einstein-Klein-Gordon systems, scalar fields coupled to matter, and the study
of equilibrium and dynamical compact configurations.

These studies form part of my broader research trajectory connecting
relativistic compact objects, scalar fields, dark matter, and gravitational
phenomenology.

## Main topics

- Einstein-Klein-Gordon systems.
- Boson-star solutions.
- Dynamical gravastar models.
- Scalar fields coupled to matter.
- Gravitational collapse and compact-object alternatives.
