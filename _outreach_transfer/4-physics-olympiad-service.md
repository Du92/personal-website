# Physics Olympiad Service

I have participated in academic service activities related to the preparation,
proposal, and selection of physics problems for Physics Olympiad initiatives.

These activities connect advanced physics training with education, mentoring,
and problem design for talented students.

## Activities

- Member of the working group involved in problem preparation for the Castilla
  y León Regional Physics Olympiad.
- Member of the jury involved in the preparation and selection of problems for
  the Ibero-American Physics Olympiad.
- Contribution to educational problem design and scientific training
  activities.
