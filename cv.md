# Curriculum Vitae

I am a final-year PhD researcher in theoretical and computational astrophysics
at the University of Salamanca. My academic profile combines compact-object
physics, dark-matter phenomenology, gravitational-wave signatures, axion/ALP
physics, numerical modeling, scientific software development, teaching, and
science communication.

[Download full CV]({{ "/assets/cv/cv.pdf" | relative_url }}){: .button }

## Academic profile

- Final-year PhD researcher at the University of Salamanca.
- Research experience at Paris Observatory and ICIMAF.
- First-author publications in relativistic astrophysics and astroparticle physics.
- Contributor to the Einstein Telescope Collaboration science case.
- Experience in teaching, outreach, scientific software, and industry-transfer projects.
